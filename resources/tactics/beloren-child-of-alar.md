# Belo'ren Child of Al'ar

## Overview
A repeating two-phase encounter in Voidlight Sanctum (VS-DR-MQD). In Phase 1, the raid fights the boss and bird adds while managing a light/void color assignment system. When the boss reaches 0 HP, it transitions to Phase 2 where players have a 30-second window to damage an egg. The cycle repeats until the egg is destroyed. A 6-minute enrage timer governs Phase 2 total time. The core challenge is correctly handling color-coded mechanics while maintaining high DPS throughput on the egg during brief burn windows.

## Phases

### Phase 1: Voidlight Convergence
- All players receive either a **light** or **void** color assignment via Voidlight Convergence
- The assigned color reduces damage taken from matching-color mechanics by 50%
- The boss uses a combination of soak mechanics, tank frontal cones, add spawns, and raid-wide pulsing damage
- Phase 1 ends when the boss reaches 0 HP and casts Death Drop, transitioning to Phase 2
- Burning Heart deals constant raid-wide pulsing damage throughout, intensifying during Rebirth windows

### Phase 2: Incubation of Flames
- The boss enters egg form; the room splits into colored cone zones
- Players have a 30-second window to deal damage to the egg
- Ashen Benediction applies a stacking 10% healing reduction after each Phase 2 cycle
- After the 30-second window, the boss returns and Phase 1 restarts
- The cycle repeats until the egg is killed or the 6-minute enrage timer expires

## Key Mechanics

### Voidlight Convergence
- **Type**: Raid-wide assignment mechanic
- **What it does**: Assigns every player either a light or void color. The assigned color reduces damage from matching-color mechanics by 50%.
- **Correct response**: Track your color assignment at all times. Position and act according to your assigned color for every subsequent mechanic.
- **Failure indicator**: Players taking unmitigated (full) damage from color mechanics they should have had 50% reduction on.
- **Common mistakes**: Losing track of color assignment mid-fight, especially after phase transitions.

### Light Dive / Void Dive
- **Type**: Raid soak mechanic
- **What it does**: Marks a single player with a colored soak circle. Deals split damage to all players inside, causes knockback, and leaves a permanent puddle on the ground.
- **Correct response**: Players with the matching color stack in the soak circle to split damage. Place the puddle at the room edges to preserve space.
- **Failure indicator**: High unsoaked damage on the marked player, puddles placed in center of room reducing available space.
- **Common mistakes**: Wrong-color players soaking (taking full damage), placing puddles in the middle of the room, insufficient players soaking.

### Infused Quills (Heroic/Mythic only)
- **Type**: Raid mechanic (targeted soak)
- **What it does**: Marks players and fires opposite-colored quills at them. A different player whose color matches the quill color must soak the damage.
- **Correct response**: Identify quill color, have a matching-color player intercept/soak each quill.
- **Failure indicator**: Unsoaked quill damage on the original target, or wrong-color player taking full damage. On Mythic, splash damage on nearby players.
- **Common mistakes**: Marked player trying to soak their own quill instead of letting a matching-color ally handle it, players standing too close on Mythic causing splash damage.

### Guardian's Edict
- **Type**: Tank mechanic (frontal cone series)
- **What it does**: The boss fires a series of colored frontal cones that deal damage and knock back players. The final cone of the combo splits between both tanks.
- **Correct response**: Only tanks soak their matching-color cone. Non-tank players must avoid all cones entirely. Tanks position to catch their assigned color cones.
- **Failure indicator**: Boss enrages if no one soaks a cone or if an opposite-color player soaks it. Non-tank players taking cone damage.
- **Common mistakes**: Tank soaking wrong-color cone triggering enrage, DPS/healers getting clipped by the frontal, tanks not positioning for the split final cone.

### Radiant Echoes
- **Type**: Raid movement/space management
- **What it does**: Orbs of both light and void colors float across the room.
- **Correct response**: Players run into orbs that match their assigned color to clear space. Avoid opposite-color orbs.
- **Failure indicator**: Damage taken from opposite-color orb collision. On Mythic, orbs exploding on contact with the boss.
- **Common mistakes**: Ignoring orbs and letting the room become cluttered, running into wrong-color orbs.

### Light Eruption / Void Eruption
- **Type**: Add interrupt mechanic (lethal if failed)
- **What it does**: Two bird adds (one light, one void) spawn and begin casting Eruption. This cast deals massive lethal raid damage if it completes.
- **Correct response**: Only players with the matching color can interrupt the Eruption cast. Assign color-matched interrupters. The birds also cast Light Blast / Void Blast as secondary casts that any player can interrupt.
- **Failure indicator**: Eruption cast completing, resulting in raid-wide lethal damage (likely a wipe). Uninterrupted Blast casts dealing avoidable damage.
- **Common mistakes**: No matching-color player ready to interrupt, confusing Eruption (color-locked) with Blast (anyone can interrupt), interrupt being on cooldown.

### Rebirth
- **Type**: Add/DPS check mechanic
- **What it does**: When bird adds die, they become eggs. The egg must be killed within 15 seconds or the add respawns at full health.
- **Correct response**: Immediately switch to and destroy the egg within 15 seconds. Burning Heart damage increases during this window.
- **Failure indicator**: Egg surviving past 15 seconds, add respawning. Elevated Burning Heart damage during Rebirth stage.
- **Common mistakes**: Slow target switching, insufficient DPS on egg, not pre-positioning for egg spawn location.

### Death Drop
- **Type**: Raid-wide knockback/damage (phase transition)
- **What it does**: When the boss reaches 0 HP in Phase 1, it smashes into the center of the room dealing damage and knocking players back. Damage is reduced by distance from center.
- **Correct response**: Move away from the center of the room before the boss reaches 0 HP.
- **Failure indicator**: Players dying or taking excessive damage from being too close to center during transition.
- **Common mistakes**: Not anticipating the phase transition, being caught in melee range at center.

### Burning Heart
- **Type**: Raid-wide passive damage (constant)
- **What it does**: Pulsing raid-wide damage throughout the entire fight. Damage increases during Rebirth windows.
- **Correct response**: Healers maintain consistent throughput. Plan healing cooldowns for Rebirth-intensified pulses.
- **Failure indicator**: Steady trickle of raid deaths not tied to any specific mechanic, deaths during Rebirth windows.
- **Common mistakes**: Healers not ramping healing during Rebirth stage, raid members not topped before Rebirth.

### Eternal Burns
- **Type**: Healer mechanic (absorb + DoT)
- **What it does**: Places an absorb shield and a DoT debuff on random players. The DoT persists until the absorb shield is fully healed through.
- **Correct response**: Healers prioritize healing through the absorb shield to remove the DoT as quickly as possible.
- **Failure indicator**: Players dying to sustained DoT damage, absorb shields persisting for extended periods.
- **Common mistakes**: Healers not prioritizing absorb targets, affected players not calling out for healing.

### Incubation of Flames
- **Type**: Raid positioning/DPS burn (Phase 2)
- **What it does**: The room splits into colored cone zones during Phase 2. Players have 30 seconds to damage the egg.
- **Correct response**: Move to the zone matching your assigned color. Use DPS cooldowns and burst the egg. Save major cooldowns for this window.
- **Failure indicator**: Players taking damage from wrong-color zones, low DPS on egg during the window.
- **Common mistakes**: Standing in wrong-color zone, not saving cooldowns for Phase 2, poor positioning wasting DPS uptime.

### Ashen Benediction
- **Type**: Raid-wide stacking debuff (Phase 2 transition)
- **What it does**: Deals a burst of fire damage to the entire raid and applies a 10% healing reduction debuff. This debuff stacks with each Phase 2 cycle and persists for the remainder of the fight.
- **Correct response**: Kill the egg as fast as possible to minimize the number of stacks accumulated. Healers must compensate for increasing healing reduction.
- **Failure indicator**: High stack count of Ashen Benediction, raid deaths to chip damage in later phases due to healing reduction.
- **Common mistakes**: Extending the fight too long leading to unhealable damage, not adjusting healing cooldown usage as stacks increase.

## Adds

### Light Bird / Void Bird
- **When**: Spawns during Phase 1 via Light/Void Eruption cast
- **Priority**: Highest priority -- interrupt Eruption immediately (color-matched players only), then interrupt Blast casts (any player), then DPS to kill
- **Abilities**:
  - **Light Eruption / Void Eruption**: Lethal raid-wide damage if cast completes. Only matching-color players can interrupt.
  - **Light Blast / Void Blast**: Secondary damage cast. Any player can interrupt.
- **Failure indicator**: Eruption cast completing (raid wipe), Blast casts going uninterrupted (avoidable damage taken), eggs from Rebirth not killed in 15 seconds (add respawns)

### Rebirth Egg
- **When**: Spawns when a bird add dies
- **Priority**: Immediate kill target -- 15-second hard timer
- **Abilities**: None (stationary)
- **Failure indicator**: Egg surviving 15 seconds, bird add respawning at full health

## Role-Specific

### Tanks
- **Guardian's Edict**: Solo soak your matching-color frontal cones. Never soak wrong-color cones or the boss enrages.
- The final cone in each Guardian's Edict combo splits between both tanks -- coordinate positioning.
- Keep the boss positioned so frontal cones do not clip the raid.
- Use active mitigation for Guardian's Edict cones and Death Drop.
- Tank swap may be needed between Edict combos depending on debuff stacks.

### Healers
- **Eternal Burns**: Prioritize healing through absorb shields to remove DoTs quickly.
- **Burning Heart**: Maintain steady healing throughput; ramp during Rebirth windows.
- **Ashen Benediction**: Plan escalating healing cooldown usage as stacks accumulate in later phases.
- Coordinate raid cooldowns for Death Drop transitions and Rebirth-intensified Burning Heart pulses.
- Dispel priorities: Eternal Burns is removed by healing through the absorb, not by dispel.

### DPS
- **Target priority**: Interrupt Eruption on adds > Kill adds > Kill Rebirth eggs (15s timer) > Boss
- **Phase 2 burn**: Save major cooldowns for Incubation of Flames 30-second egg damage windows.
- **Color awareness**: Know your assigned color for Dive soaks, Quill soaks (Heroic+), Eruption interrupts, and Radiant Echo orb clearing.
- Switch to eggs immediately on bird death -- the 15-second Rebirth timer is a hard DPS check.

## Difficulty Differences

### Normal
- Infused Quills mechanic is absent.
- Radiant Echoes orbs do not interact with the boss.
- Mechanics are less punishing overall; color mismatches deal damage but may not immediately wipe.
- Rebirth egg timer and Eruption lethality are still present but more forgiving on tuning.

### Heroic
- **Infused Quills** added: Opposite-colored quills target players, requiring a different matching-color player to soak.
- Guardian's Edict cones and damage tuning are tighter.
- Burning Heart pulsing damage is higher, making Rebirth windows more dangerous.
- Ashen Benediction stacking becomes more punishing due to higher baseline damage.
- Tighter enrage timing requires better Phase 2 burst optimization.

### Mythic
- **Infused Quills** now deal splash damage on contact, requiring soakers to be spread from the raid.
- **Radiant Echoes** orbs explode if they touch the boss (Belo'ren), adding a positioning constraint for tanks and melee.
- All damage values significantly increased across every mechanic.
- Color-mismatch penalties are more severe, potentially lethal rather than just damaging.
- Phase 2 windows demand near-perfect execution; Ashen Benediction stacks become critical faster.
- Tightest enrage timer; the egg must die within fewer Phase 2 cycles.

## Common Wipe Causes
1. **Eruption cast completes**: A Light/Void Eruption goes uninterrupted because no matching-color player was ready, dealing lethal raid damage.
2. **Guardian's Edict enrage**: Wrong-color player soaks a tank cone, or a cone goes unsoaked entirely, causing the boss to enrage.
3. **Ashen Benediction stack overflow**: Fight extends too many Phase 2 cycles, stacking healing reduction to unhealable levels.
4. **Rebirth eggs not killed**: Bird adds respawn repeatedly, overwhelming the raid with additional Eruption casts and damage.
5. **Death Drop positioning**: Players caught near center during phase transition, taking lethal proximity damage.
6. **Puddle placement (Light/Void Dive)**: Permanent puddles placed in center reducing usable room space, eventually leaving no safe areas.
7. **Eternal Burns deaths**: Healers fail to heal through absorb shields quickly enough, players die to sustained DoT damage.

## AI Analysis Notes
Key data points the AI should look for in WCL logs:
- **damage_taken entries for Guardian's Edict**: Players other than tanks taking frontal cone damage indicates positioning failures. Check for enrage buff application on boss.
- **Light Eruption / Void Eruption cast success**: Any successful Eruption cast in the log means an interrupt was missed -- correlate with matching-color player interrupt cooldown timers.
- **Light Blast / Void Blast damage_taken**: Uninterrupted secondary casts from bird adds showing avoidable damage.
- **Rebirth egg duration**: Time between egg spawn and egg death -- anything approaching or exceeding 15 seconds indicates DPS switch problems.
- **Ashen Benediction debuff stacks**: Track stack count over time. High stacks (4+) correlate strongly with late-fight wipes.
- **Burning Heart damage patterns**: Spikes during Rebirth windows indicate healers not ramping appropriately.
- **Eternal Burns debuff duration**: Long-duration Eternal Burns debuffs indicate slow healer response to absorb shields.
- **Death Drop damage_taken**: High values indicate players were too close to center during phase transition.
- **Infused Quills damage (Heroic+)**: Unsoaked quill damage on marked targets indicates failed color coordination.
- **Radiant Echoes explosion damage (Mythic)**: Orb-boss collision damage indicates poor tank positioning or orb management.
- **Death timing patterns**: Deaths clustered during Rebirth windows suggest Burning Heart + Eternal Burns combination overwhelming healers. Deaths during Phase 2 suggest wrong-color zone positioning or Ashen Benediction stack issues.
- **Color mismatch indicators**: Players taking full (unmitigated) damage from color mechanics they should have 50% reduction on -- check for Light Dive/Void Dive damage values above expected thresholds.
