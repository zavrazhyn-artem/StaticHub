Return STRICTLY a raw JSON object. No markdown wrappers, no code blocks, no trailing text.

ANALYSIS RULES:

1. MECHANIC FAILURES — For each boss, go through EVERY mechanic in the tactics. Check:
   - damage_taken: Does any ability from tactics appear? Who took the most? Is it avoidable per tactics?
   - deaths: Does the killing_blow match a mechanic from tactics? Was it avoidable?
   - debuffs: Are debuff stacks too high (indicates missed swap, failed soak, etc)?
   - interrupts: Were critical interrupts missed? How many?
   - target_damage: Are adds being killed? Who isn't switching?

2. SEVERITY CLASSIFICATION:
   - critical: Caused wipes or multiple deaths. Interrupt miss rate >50% on must-interrupt ability. Tank swap failures.
   - major: Repeated failures by same player (3+ times). Significant avoidable damage.
   - minor: Occasional failures, low damage impact.

3. PER-PLAYER DATA — For every player in participants:
   - Map ALL their deaths to specific mechanics using killing_blow + tactics
   - Classify each death as avoidable or unavoidable based on tactics
   - Calculate their add_damage_pct from target_damage.per_player
   - Check their interrupt contribution
   - Check consumable usage
   - Identify their #1 weakness and #1 strength

4. ADD PERFORMANCE — For every add type in target_damage:
   - Cross-reference with tactics to determine kill priority
   - Identify players who dealt very low add damage (not switching)
   - Note if add-related wipes occurred

5. CONSUMABLE AUDIT — Compare consumable_buffs against raid_size:
   - avg_players_per_fight << raid_size = missing consumables
   - Call out specific gaps (food, augment runes, vantus runes)

6. TOP/WORST PERFORMERS — Based on composite score:
   - Top: high parse + low deaths + good interrupt/add contribution + consumables
   - Worst: low parse + many avoidable deaths + no interrupts + no add switching + no consumables

IMPORTANT: The output must contain ALL players from the participants list in per_player_data. Do not skip anyone. Pass through the localization block unchanged.
